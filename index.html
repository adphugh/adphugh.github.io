About

My name is Adam Hughes. I grew up in the southwest suburbs of Chicago before I graduated high school and moved to Bloomington to attend Indiana University. Throughout my college career, I developed not only an affection towards the problem-solving that comes with developing solutions, but also how people interact and react to their experiences using technology. I quickly realized that the user's experience is just as important as writing functional code, if not more.

I learned this through the many hats I had in college, including being a technical consultant and later project manager for ServeIT, a non-profit consulting firm devoted to helping local non-profits with any technical needs that they normally could not afford to build. I was a teaching assistant for a 300-level web development course, assisting in teaching students the advanced usage of HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, and responsive design. I was even a tour guide showing prospective freshmen the everyday life of an Indiana University student in the residence halls!

All of this prepared me for the start of my career in Chicago at Fruition Partners, a DXC Technology Company where I joined as a ServiceNow Technical Consultant. I configured and implemented multiple projects for clients, including implementations of customer service management, IT service management, and asset management. Within my first year with DXC Fruition, I was a techncial lead for two separate client projects. I have also integrated scoped applications from the ServiceNow store for clients as well, proving my versatility and aptitude to clients and coworkers at a young age.

Outside of work, I enjoy keeping up with web development as a hobbyist whenever I get a clever or crazy idea to run with. I also enjoy playing soccer and ultimate Frisbee with my friends, as well as trying out new restaurants in Chicago!

If you made it this far, feel free to explore some of my projects below and to contact me via my LinkedIn and/or email with any questions, comments, tips, or collaborative projects you have available!

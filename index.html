About

Hello! My name is Adam Hughes. I grew up in the southwest suburbs of Chicago before I graduated high school and moved to Bloomington to attend Indiana University. Throughout my college career, I developed not only an affection towards the problem-solving that comes with creating solutions, but also how people interact and react to their experiences using technology.I quickly learned that the user's experience is just as important as writing functional code.

My many jobs in college prepared me for my homecoming to Chicago to start my career in ServiceNow consulting. I have configured and implemented multiple deployments for a variety of clients as an associate and senior technical consultant.

Outside of work, I enjoy keeping up with web development as a hooby whenever I get a clever or goofy idea to run with. I also enjoy trying out new restaurants in Chicago and watching Liverpool Football Club win trophies!

Feel free to explore some of my projects below and to contact me via my LinkedIn and/or email with any questions, comments, tips, or collaborative projects you have available!

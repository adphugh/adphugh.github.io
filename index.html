About

My name is Adam Hughes, and I am a senior at Indiana University studying informatics with a focus in Human-Centered Computing and minor in Sociology. Over the course of my college career, I have discovered a strong interest in how people interact with technology and how those interactions can be improved. I have completed projects in user experience design during some of my classes, as well as user interface development for many companies and nonprofits around the Midwest.

In this 2017-2018 school year, my senior year, I am a university instructor for a web development class that teaches front-end skills including CSS, JavaScript, and other useful plugins! I am also leading an agile team of designers and developers for the ServeIT Clinic, which helps nonprofits in the Bloomington area with any technical needs that they cannot afford on their own.

After graduation in May, I will be starting my career as a technical consultant at DXC Technology in Chicago, Illinois. I hope to gain a lot more experience in the tech industry there, while also helping my customers and developing worthwhile connections and friendships.

Feel free to explore some of my projects below and to contact me via my LinkedIn and/or email with any questions, comments, tips, or projects you have available!
